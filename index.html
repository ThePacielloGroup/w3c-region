w3c-region example

An alternative semantic custom element for headingless article/section elements used to wrap child section and/or article elements.

Old Skool

<div class="region">

Middle Skool

<div role="region">

OldNew Skool

<div is="w3c-region">

Brand New Skool

<w3c-r>

Usage

is="w3c-region"

<div is="w3c-region">
<section>
<h2>Old Skool</h2>
<p><code>&lt;div class=&quot;region&quot;&gt;</code></p>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Middle Skool</h2>
<p><code>&lt;div role=&quot;region&quot;&gt;</code></p>
</section>
</div>

<w3c-r>

<w3c-r>
<section>
<h2>OldNew Skool</h2>
<p><code>&lt;div is=&quot;w3c-region&quot;&gt;</code></p>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Brand New Skool</h2>
<p><code>&lt;w3c-r&gt;</code></p>
</section>
</w3c-r>
